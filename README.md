# Zomato SQL Business Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing business performance using SQL. The analysis covers customer behavior, product performance, revenue trends, and loyalty program impact.

---

## 🧠 Business Problems Solved

### 🔹 Level 1 — Business Scale & Growth
- Total users
- Total orders
- Total revenue
- Average order value (AOV)
- Revenue trend over time
- Order trend over time

### 🔹 Level 2 — Product Performance
- Revenue per product
- Most ordered product
- Product contribution to revenue

### 🔹 Level 3 — Customer Behavior
- Revenue per user
- First purchase date
- Repeat vs new customers

### 🔹 Level 4 — Gold Membership Impact
- Gold vs non-gold users
- Revenue comparison
- Orders before vs after gold signup

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Revenue is not consistent across years
- Sharp drop observed in 2018
- Business shows unstable growth pattern
- Few users contribute majority of revenue
